Trash to Treasures is a platform created to share ideas,experiences and innovation that the people have around the global regarding sustainable waste
management.Moreover to have debates, exchange of dialogues and global collaboration towards waste management. The innovative ideas, and experiences
gathered will be utilized to bring about sustainable waste management changes in Bhutan and in the nation of the team members.

As stated by Abraham Lincoln(16th U.S president) "Democracy is of the people,by the people and for the people" similarly, this platform is for the people
(for the sustainable souls) towards sustainable waste management.

Objectives

  • educate communities regarding proper waste management inorder to reduce waste production.
  • to create a platform for the people(for debates,exchange of dialogues and innovatives idea ) towards sustainable waste management.
  • to promote commercialization of the waste materials which have other potential uses(through sharing ideas on upcycling,recycling and reusing)the waste
    products.
  • to contribute towards green economic growth.
  • to disribute the product made out of waste to the communities in the country(especially to the rural areas).
