Creating a sustainable waste management society by the sustainable souls

The team of sustainabe waste managers will be providing advocacy programs and conducting campaigns in our local communities.
Competition are to be organized to find and explore innovative methods and instruments that can be use to manage the waste
Moreover, proper assessment of waste management will be given the most priority to manage it efficiently.Waste are the
product of underuse of the potential material thus, the team will finds ways to repurpose it for sustainable uses.

Further, a donation drive will be organized inorder to acquire monetary support to uncycle and recycle the waste into
other potential goods which will be sold to the intersted buyers and the fund generated will be used to support the Development
of our rural communities in the country.
